About Us

The values and purpose of our company is to bring family and community together through one simple message get outside and play! We want to see people of all ages, abilities and circumstances engage and connect through real life, old fashioned backyard games.

FUN & GAMES

The values and purpose of our company is to bring family and community together through one simple message get outside and play! We want to see people of all ages, abilities and circumstances engage and connect through real life, old fashioned backyard games. We know times have changed but as a nod to the way things were, we wanted to recreate our childhood, and share it with others. So, we did.

LOVE & CARE

We have put our own unique twist on traditional outdoor games like Cornhole and Kubb. Lovingly crafted from sustainable New Zealand pine, our games will become a cherished keepsake for years to come

TAKE ME WITH YOU

For Cornhole lovers who can't get enough, our aluminium alternative to the classic wooden bag toss game is easy to fold, light to carry, perfect for camping, to take to the beach or for those on the road. Personalise your set, by adding your favourite picture or photo, or for business, add your company logo for fundraising tournaments, corporate functions, and community events.

GIVING BACK

We are a family-owned business with strong ties to our local community. Empowering and supporting people is important to us, and we are proud to sponsor I am Hope and Gumboot Friday. Our games are designed to share and make memories with others. We hope you enjoy playing as much as we still do.
